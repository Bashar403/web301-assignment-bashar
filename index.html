Bashar's Egyptian Adventures

Cairo

Capital of Egypt, known for its rich history and the Great Pyramids of Giza.

Luxor

Often called the world's greatest open-air museum, Luxor offers access to ancient Pharaonic history.

Alexandria

The Pearl of the Mediterranean, famous for its historic library and beautiful beaches.

Aswan

A city of significant archaeological sites and the gateway to the temples of Philae.

Sharm El-Sheikh

A city known for its clear waters, coral reefs, and luxury resorts. A perfect destination for diving enthusiasts.

Hurghada

Renowned for its diving opportunities, Hurghada is a leading coastal resort on the Red Sea.

Local Cuisine

Experience the rich flavors of Egyptian cuisine, from traditional Koshari to delicious Shawarma.

Cultural Experiences

Engage with Egypt's vibrant culture through music, dance, and historical exploration.

Hurghada

Renowned for its diving opportunities, Hurghada is a leading coastal resort on the Red Sea.
